About

Unbreakable is a browser extension that generates cryptographically secure passwords entirely on your device. Unlike online password generators, Unbreakable makes zero network requests - your passwords never leave your device.

How to Use

  1. Click the ğŸ”’ extension icon in your toolbar
  2. Adjust password length and character types
  3. Click "Generate Password"
  4. Click "Copy" to copy to clipboard
  5. Paste into your account

Keyboard Shortcut

Press Ctrl+Shift+P (or Cmd+Shift+P on Mac) to open the generator quickly.

Verify Privacy

Don't trust us. Verify yourself:

  1. Open the extension popup
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  3. Go to the Network tab
  4. Generate 10, 50, or 100 passwords
  5. See ZERO network requests

That's proof. Your passwords never leave your device.
